Game Rules

Water Management
	•	A D – Move left and right
	•	SPACE – Move up
	•	E – Move water down

Air Management
	•	W A S D – Move up, down, left, right

Ice Management
	•	A D – Move left and right
	•	Can break ice walls

Other Controls
	•	1 2 3 – Change mode
	•	ESC – Close game
	•	R – Restart

Keys and Doors
	•	Green Key – Opens the green door
	•	Red Key – Starts the timer; if the timer runs out, the red door closes
